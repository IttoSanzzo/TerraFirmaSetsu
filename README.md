### Introduction

TerraFirmaSetsu is a TerraFirmaCraft-based modpack developed as a personal hobby project, originally created for private use by the SetsuTeaParty community.  
It is now publicly available for anyone interested in experimenting with a survival experience grounded in TerraFirmaCraft, seasoned with technological and magical progression systems.

---

## üåç Project Vision

I aimed to deliver a grounded yet immersive survival experience built on TerraFirmaCraft‚Äôs core philosophy, while remaining broad enough to accommodate different playstyles and interests.

The modpack is designed to support multiple progression paths, including:

- **Magical branches**, featuring mods such as Mana and Artifice, Botania, Hexerei, Malum, Blood Magic, and others
- **Technical and industrial progression**, with mods like Immersive Engineering, Create, Thermal Expansion, and similar systems

These paths are intended to coexist rather than compete, allowing players to specialize or combine approaches as they see fit.
